3D Printing in the makerspace

3D Printing Policies

1. Please leave 3D prints with 3+ hours of printing time to run overnight or over the weekend.

2. On that note, if a 3D print (especially on the Bambus!) is insanely long, please consider other avenues of fabrication.

3. Upon starting the 3D print, please stay and monitor the print until it has started printing successfully.

4. Please put away all 3D printer spools once you are done with them in the filament shelf.

5. Please do not touch the build plate. This will dirty the build plate and stop the print from sticking to the plate.

6. Please pay attention to which printer you are booking

7. Booking Form takes precedence over other prints going on, so if you have trouble with starting your print, please alert staff, and we can figure things out from there.

8. Please read the fine print in the booking invite! It's very useful and important.
